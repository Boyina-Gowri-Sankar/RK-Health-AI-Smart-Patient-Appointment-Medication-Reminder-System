# Conclusion

# The RK Health – Smart Patient Appointment & Medication Reminder System successfully demonstrates how web technologies and Artificial Intelligence can be integrated to develop an efficient healthcare management solution. The system enables users to manage doctor appointments, medication schedules, health records, and reminders through a centralized, responsive dashboard. By integrating Google Apps Script, Google Sheets, Twilio SMS, Google Calendar, and Groq AI, the application provides automated reminders, cloud-based data storage, AI-generated health summaries, and easy access to patient information.

# The project also highlights the benefits of AI in healthcare by converting medical notes into simple, patient-friendly summaries, improving user understanding and engagement. Its cloud-based architecture ensures easy deployment, scalability, and accessibility across devices. Overall, RK Health offers a practical and cost-effective solution for personal healthcare management while providing a strong foundation for future enhancements such as secure user authentication, wearable device integration, predictive analytics, and advanced healthcare recommendations.
